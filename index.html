Intro

I have spent the last three months working on a Data analytics bootcamp through Thinkful, where I have been able to focus my interests in data science. I have spent my time working on projects in excel, sql, python and tableau, creating interactive dashboards and discovering the benefits and drawbacks of each system. Additionally, I have become proficient navigating unix terminal where I can create files and directories and familiar with remote login.

I am very interested in combining data analysis and presentation skills to help solve real business needs. I am self-motivated and enthusiastic team player with a strong level of attention to detail and problem solving abilities. Check out my work.
